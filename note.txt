Exactly! You nailed the logic. Let's look at the "Why" behind using a map specifically.

If you are drinking the file through a "straw" (the Scanner) one line at a time, you have a problem: The computer has a very short memory. Once the straw moves to Line 2, it completely forgets what was on Line 1.

The Map is your "Notebook" where you write down what you saw so you don't forget.
